I was born in the Philippines and immigrated the the US when I was 4 years old. As a kid I was very energetic, but have mellowed out as I aged. Despite being energetic, I was incredibly shy and found it hard to socialize well. I had friends, but it was difficult getting to know new people. As I grew up I may have simmered down in terms of the amount of energy I had. However, I also began to get better with speaking to people that I didn't know. Now I am completely comfortable with talking to strangers and getting to know new people.

I have one sister and one brother, and I am the youngest between the three of us. I also have a pet dog. Her name is Panda because she kind of looks link a panda (Great naming there). We've had her for about nine years now and she's getting up there in age, which I am saddened by.

I am a very cheap man. I am not lying. I am a very cheap man. I go out of my way to spend as little money as I possibly can, and I like to surround myself with similar people. There is no story to perfectly illustrate this as the time some friends and I went to watch a movie. When we left the theater we noticed it had started to rain. Instead of us eating lunch at a place next to the theater, we walked about a mile to get food that was a few dollars cheaper. The rain intensified as we walked and by the time we arrived, we are drenched head to toe in water.
